About the Club

Explore global commodities markets.

Commodities connect geopolitics, economics, industry, technology, and everyday life.

Why We Exist

A practical introduction to a major part of finance.

Commodity markets affect the prices of energy, food, transportation, construction materials, and manufactured goods. They are also deeply connected to inflation, international trade, and geopolitical risk.

Our club creates a place for students to build market knowledge through structured learning, regular discussion, research, and competition preparation.

Club Goals

What we are building.

Learn

Make commodities markets approachable for students without prior experience.

Research

Publish clear market notes and build a shared knowledge base over time.

Compete

Prepare members for undergraduate commodities and trading competitions.

Who Should Join?

You do not need to be a finance major.
